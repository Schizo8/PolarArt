About Me

Welcome to my art portfolio! My name is Polar, and I am a digital artist who specializes in military aesthetics, while also exploring the personal and emotional aspects of my art. Through my work, I aim to capture the human element of various environments and situations, particularly those of individuals who are subject to their surroundings. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy my art as much as I enjoy creating it.
